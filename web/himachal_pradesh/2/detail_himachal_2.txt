Day 1: arrival to Manali 
Arrival in Delhi.
Volvo coach pick-up from Majnu ka tilla /Shrilanka house, New Delhi Railway station at 5:30 p.m.
Transfer to Manali by evening.
Reach Manali at 8:00 to 9:00 a.m. 

Day 2: Manali Local Sightseeing
Reach Manali at 09:00 AM in the morning.
Meet with our local representative at Manali Volvo Stand.
Pick-up and transfer to Manali Hotel.
Check-in the hotel, rest and freshen up.
In the afternoon, visit Hadimba Devi Temple, Manu Temple, Club House, Shiva Temple, Tibetan Monastery, Van Vihar, Vashisht Village, Hot spring Water, etc.
Stroll down the Mall Road in the evening.
Dinner and overnight stay at hotel.

Day 3: Manali (Solang Valley) and Gulab (Up To Possible Snow Point)
After breakfast, start for Solang Valley.
Solang Valley is a side valley at the top of the Kullu valley in Himachal Pradesh. It is known for its summer and winter sport conditions. The sports most commonly offered are Ropeway, parachuting, paragliding, skating and zorbing. Snow melts during the summer months starting May, and skiing is then replaced by zorbing.
Dinner & overnight stay in Manali.

Day 4: Manali to Kullu and Manikaran (Kasol)- 85 Kms
After breakfast, drive to Kullu.
Enroute visit Vaishno Devi Temple and Kullu shawls factory.
Head to Manikaran.
Manikaran is located in the Parvati Valley between the rivers Vyaas and Parvati in the Kullu district of Himachal Pradesh. The hot water springs of Manikaran are famous across the country for their healing properties. The temperature of water is more than the boiling point making it possible to boil rice, pulses etc. in it.
Reach back to Manali in the evening.
Dinner and overnight stay at hotel.

Day 5: Manali to Delhi
After breakfast, check-out from hotel at 12:00 noon.
Explore the local market (you can keep your luggage at hotel).
Drop at Volvo bus stand at 4:30 PM for onward journey to Delhi.
