2 Nights / 3 Days
Vadodara(2N)
price = 7000/-



Day 1: Arrival in Vadodara
On arrival at Vadodara, you will be picked up from the airport/railway station.
Transfer to the hotel and check-in.
The evening is free for you to take the Vadodara city tour.
Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 2: Excursion to Kevadia
After breakfast, proceed to Kevadia.
Visit Statue of Unity - the world's tallest statue, Narmada Dam, and Poicha Temple. 
Return to Vadodara.
Overnight stay at the hotel.

Day 3: Departure
After breakfast, check out from the hotel and transfer to Vadodara airport or railway station for your onward journey back home.
